Founded in 1994, by Len Visconti and Rino Guida, Gempac has been redefining service and quality ever since and is known in the industry for its clean, quality work at competitive prices. Integrity, professionalism, reliability and quality are the cornerstone of Gempac`s success and longevity. Relationship building and people are what count. The Gempac team recognizes that in this increasingly competitive market, only companies with proven reliability, service and superior workmanship will succeed. To ensure this continued success, we at Gempac listen carefully to our clients` needs and work closely with them to achieve 100% satisfaction. Our portfolio includes projects with property management companies, insurance companies, interior designers, engineering firms, construction companies, architects, builders, private condominium and residential homeowners.
