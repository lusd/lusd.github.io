The Flying Phobia Master program...

The Flying Phobia Master program contains a powerful anxiety-releasing guided imagery therapy audio recording.

Guided imagery is a therapeutic technique in which you listen to simple instructions and visualize a calm, stress-free experience of something that currently brings you anxiety and fear.

With practice, your mind will substitute the negative feelings you experience over the thought of flying with positive ones, allowing you to finally fly without fear.
